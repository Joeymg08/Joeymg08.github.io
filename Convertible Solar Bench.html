Convertible Bench/Table

As a capstone project for my senior year of high school, I modeled what my team and I simply couldn't put down on paper. Tasked with solving a problem using STEM, we conceptualized a convertible bench to table design with a built-in distress button in cases of emergency. After modelling the entire structure, my team and I were able to build the solar bench to the exact specifications we needed.
